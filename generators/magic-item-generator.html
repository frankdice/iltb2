--- layout: page title: Magic Item Generator ---

This tool randomly generates D&D 5e items with (mostly) weak and (hopefully) unique magical enchantments. I hope this helps when your players want to loot the body of random goblin #3.

The generator randomly selects from lists of items and magical effects for a total of 31,977 possible items. Select or deselect the categories below to generate specific types of items. A CSV file with the complete list of magic effects can be downloaded here.
